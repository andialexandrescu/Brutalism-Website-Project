About Me

THE BOLD HORIZON is a website project developed by Andra Alexandrescu, using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I'm a first-year student pursuing a Computer Science degree at The University of Bucharest. I have hands-on experience coding in various programming languages like Python, C++, Assembly, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Outside of coding, I'm an avid skier and swimmer. I enjoy listening to music of any kind, taking long walks, travelling, photography, editing, and watching classic Romanian and French comedy films. Thanks to some of the interests I've already mentioned, I have developed a deep appreciation for Brutalist Architecture.
